Aboute TO Server

Gta interdiction server is the first Arabian server of its kind that relies primarily on gameplay similar to FIVE M,
after developing its own SAMP and data.
The Gta interdiction server also includes a more realistic gameplay than any other Arabian server SAMP server.
For example, when buying a car, forget about it disappearing from the garage, as is the traditional way.
You have to go to the garage to remove it here in the Gta interdiction Server.
A second idea: once you buying the car, you must maintain it and use it properly,
because it will remain in the city when you leave SAMP
